Ordering Dependent Tasks

Check

Dependencies

Click on the coloured boxes below for more detailed feedback.

  • Green tasks
    must happen first, in the correct order.
    • The Lot must be prepared before the Foundation can be laid.
    • The Fountation must be laid before the Wall Frames can be constructed.
  • Orange tasks
    happen second, in any order.
    • Plumbing and Wiring can be installed once the Wall Frames are done.
    • Exterior walls can be constructed once the Wall Frames are done.
    • Roof Frames can be constructed once the Wall Frames are done.
  • Red tasks
    happen third, careful about the order.
    • Roof can be covered once the Roof Frames are done.
    • Interior Walls can be constructed once the Roof is covered.
    • Interior can be finished once the Interior walls are constructed.
    • Exterior can be finished once the Roof is covered.
  • Grey tasks
    are out of scope, put these last.
    • A swimming pool can be added after the house is done.
    • A back pergola would be nice to have.
    • A double garage would be nice, but is not required.
